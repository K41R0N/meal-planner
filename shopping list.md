# Essential Kitchen Ingredients

## Monthly/Long-term Staples
**Oils & Fats:**
- Coconut Oil (preferably organic)
- Olive Oil (first cold-pressed)
- Clarified Butter (Ghee)
- Nut Butters (Macadamia, Cashew, Almond)
- Sesame Oil

**Nuts & Seeds:**
- Almonds
- Cashews
- Pecans
- Macadamias
- Walnuts/Pine Nuts
- Sesame Seeds
- Sunflower Seeds
- Chia Seeds
*(Soak nuts before use to reduce lectins)*

**Dry Goods:**
- Coconut Cream
- Sago Flour
- Rice Flour
- Brown Rice
- Dried Coconut
- Rice Pasta
- Nutritional Yeast

**Spices & Seasonings:**
- Sea Salt
- Himalayan Salt
- Spices (Cinnamon, Turmeric, Black Pepper, Oregano, Thyme, Curry, Cumin)
- White/Apple Vinegar
- Soy Sauce
- Mustard

**Sweeteners & Specialty:**
- Dates
- Cacao
- Organic Panela
- Powdered Panela
- Acai Powder
- Tahini (Sesame Paste)
- Honey
- Coconut Extract
- Moringa
